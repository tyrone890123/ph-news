Design variants — sources in footers

  • A — Source in story footer

    Closest to the current design. Headlines become plain text; each story ends with a quiet "Read at [Source] · category · time" line where the source name is the only link.

  • B — Sources as endnotes

    Print-style. Headlines carry a small superscript number; all source links are collected in a numbered "Sources" block in the page footer, like a broadsheet citation list.

  • C — Story card with source bar

    Each story becomes a hairline-bordered card whose bottom bar is its footer: source link on the left, category and time on the right. Trades the rule-separated list for cards.
